**Adaptive Software Development (ASD)** is an **iterative and Agile software development approach** that focuses on **adapting to changing requirements**, continuous learning, and delivering high-quality software in complex and uncertain environments. Unlike traditional models, ASD assumes that **requirements and solutions evolve** as the project progresses.

---

### **Key Features of Adaptive Software Development (ASD)**

1. Emphasizes **adaptation over prediction**.
2. Encourages **collaboration and learning** among team members.
3. Iterative and incremental delivery of software.
4. Suitable for **complex, high-change projects** where exact requirements are unclear.
5. Focuses on **risk management through short cycles** and feedback loops.

---

### **Three Phases of ASD**

ASD organizes the development process into **three main phases**, sometimes called the **“Speculate-Collaborate-Learn” cycle**:

| Phase              | Description                                             | Key Activities                                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Speculate**   | Planning and envisioning the project in a flexible way. | - Understand business vision and objectives. <br> - Create an initial plan, but allow it to evolve. <br> - Identify high-level requirements and constraints.         |
| **2. Collaborate** | Building the software through iterative teamwork.       | - Developers, testers, and customers work closely. <br> - Iterative development and prototyping. <br> - Focus on delivering functional components quickly.           |
| **3. Learn**       | Reflecting on the results and adapting future work.     | - Review and assess completed iterations. <br> - Gather feedback from customers and stakeholders. <br> - Update plans, designs, and requirements for the next cycle. |

**Explanation:**

* ASD is **cyclical**, with each iteration refining the software and the plan.
* The **Speculate-Collaborate-Learn** loop allows teams to **adapt to changes** and improve continuously.

---

✅ **In short:** ASD is ideal for **complex projects with uncertain requirements** because it emphasizes **learning, collaboration, and adaptability** rather than rigid planning.
